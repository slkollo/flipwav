Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

FlipWAV is a 100% browser-based audio converter. Your files are never uploaded to any server. All processing happens locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

FlipWAV does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or audio files. The converter runs entirely within your web browser using JavaScript.

We do not require account registration, login, or any personal details to use the service.

2. Audio Files

All audio files you upload are processed locally in your browser. Files are never sent to our servers or any third-party servers. Once you close or refresh the page, all file data is cleared from memory.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

FlipWAV uses browser localStorage only to save:

  • Your conversion history (file names and dates only — no file content)
  • Your preferred theme (dark or light mode)

This data stays on your device and is never transmitted anywhere. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser data.

4. Analytics & Advertising

FlipWAV uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your browsing history. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information: policies.google.com/privacy

We may use anonymous analytics tools to understand general site traffic patterns. No personally identifiable information is collected.

5. Third-Party Libraries

FlipWAV uses the following open-source libraries loaded from CDN:

  • lamejs — MP3 encoding
  • JSZip — ZIP file creation
  • FileSaver.js — file download
  • ffmpeg.wasm — M4A encoding

These libraries run locally in your browser and do not send your files to any external servers.

6. Children's Privacy

FlipWAV does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The service is intended for general audiences.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the About page.
