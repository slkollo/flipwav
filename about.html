About FlipWAV

Free · Private · Browser-Based Audio Converter

FlipWAV is a free, browser-based audio converter that lets you convert audio files between multiple formats instantly — with no uploads, no sign-ups, and no waiting. Everything runs locally on your device.

Whether you're a musician, game developer, podcaster, or just need to convert a quick audio file, FlipWAV makes it fast and easy.

Supported Formats

MP3 WAV OGG FLAC M4A AAC WEBM AIFF

Features

100% Private
Your files never leave your device. All conversion happens locally in your browser.
Batch Convert
Upload and convert up to 50 files at once. Download all as a single ZIP file.
No Signup
No account, no email, no registration required. Just open and convert.
Per-File Bitrate
Set individual bitrate for each file. Full control over output quality.
Audio Preview
Play and preview any audio file directly in the browser before converting.
Dark & Light Mode
Switch between dark and light themes. Preference is saved automatically.

How It Works

FlipWAV uses Web Audio API and open-source libraries (lamejs, ffmpeg.wasm) to encode and decode audio entirely within your browser. No server processing. No file uploads. Your audio stays on your device at all times.
